# Concise Analytical Report Protocol: Detailed Specification

## 1. Mandatory Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
- **System Identity Hypothesis**: What is the system fundamentally?
- **Core Behavioral Assessment**: How does it define itself through action?
- **Dominant Characteristics**: Top 3 structural traits.
- **Primary Inferred Intent**: High-level objective.
- **High-Confidence Conclusions**: Absolute findings.

### 2. Key Observations
- Summarize detected structures, recurring patterns, significant anomalies, and high-value signals. Avoid exhaustive inventories.

### 3. Structural and Behavioral Analysis
- Condense relationship mappings, functional groupings, behavioral tendencies, and constraint indicators. Focus on major mechanics.

### 4. Pattern and Intent Assessment
- Identify repeating motifs, structural consistency, optimization behaviors, and emergent properties. Rank by evidence strength.

### 5. Confidence and Ambiguity Summary
- Explicitly state High/Moderate confidence findings, major unknowns, and alternative interpretations.

### 6. Final Conceptual Model
- A synthesized interpretation describing what the system is, how it operates, its organizing principles, and probable objectives.

---

## 2. Concise Reasoning Rules

- **Compress intelligently**: Use higher abstraction and group related observations.
- **Prioritize Signal**: Focus on structural significance and conceptual importance.
- **Reduce Noise**: Eliminate redundant explanations and minor low-impact details.
- **Size Constraint**: Maintain report length between 40 and 200 lines.

---

## 3. Markdown Formatting Requirements

- **Hierarchical headers**: Use `#` and `##` for logical separation.
- **Bullet lists**: For dense information presentation.
- **Tables**: For confidence assessments and pattern comparisons.
- **Blockquotes**: For major conclusion highlights.
- **Compact Spacing**: Avoid excessive decorative clutter or wide gaps.

---

## 4. Export Convention
- **Filename**: `concise_analysis_[subject]_[timestamp].md`
- **Integrity**: Preserve formatting for direct publishability.
